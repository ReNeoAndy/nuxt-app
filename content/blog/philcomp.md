The Philosophy of Computing Research Group is Cool!

Welcome to a space where the mind meets the machine! The Research Group on Philosophy of Computing is dedicated to diving deep into the most fundamental questions about computationâ€”and trust us, it's fascinating.

This group isn't just about algorithms and code; it's about exploring the philosophical foundations of computing itself. We investigate everything from the theoretical bedrock of computation to its wide-ranging epistemological, ethical, and social implications. Think: What does it mean to "compute"? How does technology change what we know and how we act?

Born out of dynamic academic meetings in 2022 and 2023, this group is a continuation of a vibrant intellectual community. Our commitment is to the production of essential philosophical and scientific knowledge.
